Burned Chicken Demonlist (BCD) Rules

Joining and Submitting

Discord Membership Required:

To participate, you must join the Discord server The Real Chickens. Submit your completions there.

Note: Indicate whether you are a mobile or PC player before submitting.

Device Compatibility:

All devices (PC and mobile) are welcome.

Helpful Hacks Allowed:

Hacks like “Hitbox on Death” or “FPS Bypass” are permitted.

Video Proof Required:

Submissions must include full, video footage showing the attempt from start to completion.

Video Submission Guidelines

Recording Requirements:

  • Quality: Minimum recommended resolution is 720p.
  • Audio: Audio must not be muted. PC players should include click sounds.
  • Editing: No cuts or interruptions during the level.

Submission Format:

Submit completions in the Discord channel with:

  • Level name
  • Creator
  • Player's in-game name
  • Completion video link (YouTube link)
  • Completion date (optional)

Legitimate Gameplay Only:

  • Runs using hacks, cheats (e.g., noclip or speedhacks), or other exploits are not allowed.
  • Play Fair: Don’t use someone else’s footage or account.

Moderation and Rules Enforcement

Moderator Decisions:

  • Submissions will be reviewed by moderators, who have the final say.
  • Players can appeal rejected submissions within 48 hours by providing additional proof.

Respect the Community:

Toxic behavior, harassment, or spamming will result in removal from the server or list.

List Structure

Level Categories:

  • Main List: Top 10 most difficult demons.
  • Extended List: Challenging levels that aren’t as hard as the top 10.

Leaderboard Updates:

The leaderboard will be updated on the Discord server and the BCD website whenever a player submits a new completion.

Same-Time Submissions:

If two players submit at the same time, the first upload timestamp in Discord determines the winner.

Subsequent submissions will still appear in the levels information (player’s profile in the future) but not as the first completion.

BCD Website Policies

Video Hosting:

Videos submitted via Discord will be uploaded to the BCD website by moderators.

Player Profiles:

Feature in progress: Player profiles will display completions, submission dates, and achievements.

Cheating Reports:

  • Cheaters will be banned from the list and leaderboard.
  • Report suspected cheating to moderators with evidence.
